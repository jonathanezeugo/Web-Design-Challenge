Maximum Temperature

This is plot of Latitude location coordinates vs ambient temperature. The data does support the observations of weather patterns. It shows that the weather becomes warmer when approaching the equator at Lat. 0 degrees. The Northern hemisphere is much cooler than the Southern hemisphere, possibly because of the tilt of the earth, which makes the Southern hemisphere quite warmer relative to the Northern hemisphere. This data was obtained via API query in winter when the Southern hemisphere is much warmer. May explain observations.
